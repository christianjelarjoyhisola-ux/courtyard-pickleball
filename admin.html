SYSTEM USAGE AGREEMENT
CourtYard Pickleball Booking System — Version 1

Read the full agreement before signing. The Sign button unlocks only after you scroll to the bottom.

1. Parties

This Agreement is entered into between Jelar Joy Hisola ("System Owner"), the developer and operator of the CourtYard Pickleball Booking System, and the Court Owner identified below ("Court Owner"), who is granted access to the system for the purpose of managing pickleball court reservations in Sibagat, Agusan del Sur.

2. System Fee

For each confirmed booking processed through the system, the Court Owner agrees to pay the System Owner a system fee of ₱15.00 (Philippine Pesos, fifteen pesos) per booking. This fee covers platform hosting, fraud detection, automated receipt verification, and technical maintenance.

3. Billing Schedule

System fees are due every Sunday. The System Owner will send a weekly billing statement via GCash message or SMS to the Court Owner's registered contact number. Payment must be made to the System Owner's GCash number 09683666003 (Christian Jelar Joy Hisola) no later than 11:59 PM on the Sunday of the billing week.

4. Late Payment & Suspension

Failure to remit payment within 3 days of the weekly due date will result in suspension of the Court Owner's admin access until the outstanding balance is settled in full. Repeated non-payment (2 or more consecutive weeks) may result in permanent termination of access.

5. Fee Adjustment

The System Owner may adjust the per-booking fee with a minimum of 7 days written notice (via GCash message or email). Continued use of the system after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the updated fee.

6. Data & Confidentiality

Booking records, customer contact information, and payment data are confidential. The Court Owner agrees not to share system credentials or export customer data for purposes outside the management of the court.

7. Acceptance by Electronic Signature

Pursuant to the Philippine Electronic Commerce Act of 2000 (Republic Act No. 8792), this Agreement is legally binding when accepted via electronic signature. By typing your legal full name and drawing your signature below, and clicking "I Agree & Sign", you confirm that:

  • You have read and understood the full terms above.
  • You are the authorized Court Owner or representative.
  • You agree to the ₱15.00 per-booking system fee billed every Sunday.
  • This electronic signature carries the same legal weight as a handwritten signature under RA 8792.
